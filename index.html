Hack Homebase 2020

Join us for 48 hours of Cross-pollination. No more Storefront or Enterprise talks! (At least for these two days... ) Think of something everyone may benefit from. It could be a design, an API, or even a process that saves the team some sweat.

How to participate?

This virtual hackathon is open to all Homebase folks and includes the following 5 steps:

1. Register for participation. This helps us plan the event.

2. Share your ideas. It is encouraged to focus on Wayfair related issues, so everyone can play their part.

3. Build your team. An ideal team has 3-5 people from at least 2 functions. More perspectives add more power.

4. Start hacking! Bring your idea to life, and don't forget to check-in with our judges for feedback.

5. Deliver your pitch. Share the awesome work with the entire Homebase team through a 5-min pitch.

How do I win?

Most of all, we want you to have fun, collaborate with peers you don't normally do, put passion into the project and deliver an interesting pitch. That being said, our judges will pick one winning team based on the innovation, execution, coolness and delivery of the pitch.
