Join us this May for 48 hours of Cross-functional Collaboration.
Work out something your fellow Wayfairians may benefit from. It could be a design, an API, or a process that saves the team some sweat.

How to participate?

This virtual hackathon is open to all Homebase folks. Please keep in mind of the following important dates.

  • Complete registration by 5pm EST on 4/27.

  • From 5/10 to 5/13, submit your ideas on Miro. Focus on Wayfair related issues, so everyone can play their part.

  • Build or join a team by 5/14. An ideal team has 3-5 people from at least 2 disciplines.

  • Happy hacking! Final event dates are 5/20 and 5/21.

  • At the end of 5/21, share your great work with the Homebase team in a 5-min pitch.

How do I win?

Most of all, we want you to have fun, collaborate with peers you don't normally do, put passion into the project and deliver an interesting pitch. That being said, our judges will pick one winning team based on the innovation, execution, coolness and delivery of the pitch.
